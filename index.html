Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our products, add items to your cart, proceed to checkout, and follow the payment process. You'll receive an order confirmation email.

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard (5-7 business days), express (2-3 days), and overnight shipping options. Shipping costs vary by location and method.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unused items in original packaging. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We accept PayPal, Stripe, and Cash on Delivery. For large purchases, financing options may be available.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement. After that, contact our support team to discuss options.

How do I become a seller?

Visit our seller signup page and complete the registration process. You'll need to provide shop details, banking information, and a valid ID.
